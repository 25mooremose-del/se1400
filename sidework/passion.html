Who am I?

My name is Moses Moore, I was born in 2008 in Issaquah WA, but I grew up in Southern Utah. I have 3 siblings and I am the second oldest. As of now I am in my senior year of high school. After high school I plan to serve a two-year mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints. Outside of school I play Volleyball, watch movies, listen to music, and hang out with my friends.

Why am I creating this website?

The main reason I’m creating this website is because I’m currently enrolled in a college Web Development class — and, naturally, building a website comes with the territory. But beyond the assignment, I genuinely love creating things. I don’t want this site to be something I submit for a grade and then forget about. I want it to grow with me. Right now, it’s a passion project. In the future, I hope it becomes something like a digital time capsule — a place where I can document my thoughts, interests, and experiences, and look back in ten years to see who I was at this stage of my life. Less seriously, while looking for inspiration, I came across a personal website made by a guy named Moses Moore this website. It immediately set the bar. He is now, unofficially, my greatest rival. My mission? To build a Moses Moore website so powerful, so legendary, that there can only be one.
