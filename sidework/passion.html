Who am I?

My name is Moses Moore, I was born in 2008 in Issaquah WA, but I grew up in Southern Utah. I have 3 siblings and I am the second oldest. As of now I am in my senior year of high school. After high school I plan to serve a two-year mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints. Outside of school I play Volleyball, watch movies, listen to music, and hang out with my friends.

Why am I creating this website?

The main reason I'm making this website is because I'm enrolled in a college Web Development class, so of course I gotta make a website. But, I love creating things and after I'm done with this class I don't want this website to be done with it. So this website is my passion project right now and in the future I want it to serve almost as a journal for my life, something I can look back on in 10 years and see what my high school self was like. Less seriously, when looking for inspiration on what website to create I stumbled upon this website, which is a personal website created by another guy named Moses Moore, and is currently my biggest competition because I want my Moses Moore website to be better than his (there can only be one).
